Disclaimer

MiDispensary is a medical clinic that offers alternative medicine. Alternative medicines can affect each individual differently due to a variety of factors, and as with any medication you may experience side effects. If you do experience any side effects as a result of medication, please contact MiDispensary as soon as possible. For medical advice, always seek the guidance of a trained healthcare practitioner. Alternative medicines in Australia are accessible via the Special Access Scheme or by an Approved Authorised Prescriber and this is regulated by the Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA). You can visit the Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA) website for more information.
